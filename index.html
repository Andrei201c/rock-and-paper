Rock and Paper

Rock and Paper is a simple game, you chose one of three elements (Rock, Paper and Scissors) and your fiend (in this case your friend is the Computer) chose another element, if you chose Scissors and he chose Rock, he beat you, if you chose Scissors and he chose Paper you beat him, in case you and him chose the same element is tied. Have fun!!

To start game, you need to open Console.

If don't know how to open console press CTRL Shift J
